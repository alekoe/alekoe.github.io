I am a full professor at the École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS), in the Department of Software and IT Engineering. Before joining ÉTS, I was a full professor at PUC-PR, Brazil and an associate professor at UFPR, Brazil. I am also co-founder and former CEO of InviSys in Brazil.
I received my Ph.D. from ÉTS in 2002.
My current research focuses on:

  • affective computing
  • thrustworthy machine learning
  • multimodal machine learning
